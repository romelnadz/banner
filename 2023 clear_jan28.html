IN BETWEEN TILE FINISHES

Given the variety of alternatives, picking the right tile can take time and effort. There are standard classifications for finishes or other effects added to a tile's surface that alter its appearance, namely, Glossy, Matte, and Rustic. To choose the best tiles, let's examine the relevance of tile surfaces in greater detail.

GET GLEAM WITH GLOSSY

Glossy refers to a bright, reflective surface that can give the interior a neat, sophisticated appearance.

  • Dirt and stain resistant
  • Low maintenance
  • Make the most out of lighting
  • Make the space seem bigger and brighter

SAFE SURFACE WITH MATTE

Matte is a surface that occupies a medium ground. It creates a natural, cozy, yet fashionable appeal in terms of style.

  • Conceals stains and smudges
  • Minimum maintenance
  • Less Slippery
  • Non-reflective surface that is slightly granular

NO SLIP WITH RUSTIC

Outdoor spaces frequently include rustic furnishings because of their highly textured and rough appearance. Typically, it has earth tones that give any space an air of natural beauty.

  • Slip resistant
  • High maintenance
  • For wet and outdoor areas
  • Creates a natural look
